Grilled Cheese

Return

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat skillet over medium heat.
  2. Generously butter one side of two slices of bread.
  3. Place bread butter-side-down onto skillet bottom and add 1 slice of cheese, placing the other slice of bread butter-side-up on top.
  4. Grill until lightly browned and flip over; continue grilling until cheese is melted.
